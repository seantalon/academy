## Fight Overview

**Location:** Violet Sanctum (Crystalline Caverns, Level -7)  
**Health:** 15,000,000 HP  
**Enrage Timer:** 12 minutes  
**Recommended Composition:** 2 tanks, 4-6 healers, 12-14 DPS

<Aside type="caution" title="Prerequisites">
- Minimum level 85
- Completed "The Purple Prophecy" questline  
- At least 8 players (recommended 12-20)
- Purple Resistance gear recommended
</Aside>

## Phase Breakdown

<Tabs syncKey="phase-guides">
<TabItem label="Phase 1 (100-70%)" icon="approve-check">

### The Awakening

Zorbulon begins the fight in a relatively docile state but becomes increasingly aggressive.

**Key Mechanics:**

- **Void Pulse** every 15 seconds
- **Purple Rain** every 45 seconds  
- **Mind Rend** on random players every 30 seconds
- **Tentacle Slam** on tanks every 20 seconds

**Strategy:**

- Tanks should taunt swap after each Tentacle Slam
- Spread out during Purple Rain - meteors have 8-yard impact radius
- When Mind Rend targets a player, all others must break line of sight immediately
- Healers focus on dispelling Void Touched debuffs when possible

</TabItem>

<TabItem label="Phase 2 (70-30%)" icon="warning">

### The Fury

Zorbulon's true power awakens, and the arena transforms with floating purple crystals.

**New Mechanics:**

- **Crystal Prison** - Traps 3 random players in purple crystals
- **Dimensional Rift** - Opens portals that spawn adds
- **Psychic Scream** - Raid-wide fear effect

**Strategy:**

- Assign DPS to quickly destroy Crystal Prisons (30k HP each)
- Position raid to avoid Dimensional Rifts (they persist for 20 seconds)
- Use fear immunity cooldowns for Psychic Scream
- Rifts spawn 2-3 "Void Spawn" adds that must be killed quickly

</TabItem>

<TabItem label="Phase 3 (30-0%)" icon="rocket">

### The Transcendence

Zorbulon attempts to merge with the void, becoming increasingly unstable.

**Phase Changes:**

- All previous abilities become more frequent and deadly
- **Reality Collapse** - Room sections become uninhabitable
- **Void Cascade** - Chain damage that spreads between players
- **Final Gambit** - Enrage at 5% health

**Strategy:**

- Stay grouped but not stacked for Void Cascade
- Move as a raid to avoid Reality Collapse zones
- Use all raid cooldowns and bloodlust during Final Gambit
- Healers must be ready for massive spike damage

</TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Role-Specific Strategies

<Tabs syncKey="role-strategies">
<TabItem label="Tank Strategy" icon="approve-check">

### Tank Responsibilities

<Steps>
1. **Tentacle Slam Management** - Taunt swap after each slam
2. **Positioning** - Keep boss near crystals for LoS breaks
3. **Cooldown Usage** - Use defensives before slams, not after
4. **Communication** - Call out taunt swaps clearly
</Steps>

:::caution[Tank Warning]
Use defensive cooldowns before Tentacle Slam, not after. The knockback and stun leave you vulnerable.
:::

</TabItem>

<TabItem label="Healer Priorities" icon="heart">

### Healing Strategy

**Priority Order:**
1. Tank healing during Tentacle Slams
2. Dispelling Void Touched debuffs
3. Raid healing for Void Pulse
4. Emergency healing for Crystal Prison victims

**Cooldown Usage:**
- **Phase 1:** Conserve major cooldowns
- **Phase 2:** Use cooldowns for add phases
- **Phase 3:** Rotate all major healing cooldowns

:::tip[Healer Tip]
Keep HoTs running during high-damage phases and save dispels specifically for Void Touched debuffs.
:::

</TabItem>

<TabItem label="DPS Responsibilities" icon="rocket">

### DPS Priorities

**Phase 1:**
- Primary: Boss damage
- Secondary: Breaking Crystal Prisons when they appear

**Phase 2:**
- <Badge text="1" variant="danger" /> Crystal Prisons (immediate break)
- <Badge text="2" variant="caution" /> Void Spawn adds from rifts
- <Badge text="3" variant="note" /> Boss damage

**Phase 3:**
- Save burst cooldowns for this phase
- Focus boss while avoiding Reality Collapse
- Use movement abilities wisely

:::caution[DPS Warning]
Save burst cooldowns for Crystal Prison breaks - trapped players take increasing damage over time.
:::

</TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Tips for Success

<Aside type="tip" title="Pro Strategies">

**Preparation:**
- Mark safe zones with world markers before pull
- Assign Crystal Prison breakers by group
- Practice movement patterns during breaks

**Execution:**
- Use voice countdown for important mechanics
- Keep communication clear and concise  
- Save battle resurrections for Phase 3

**Positioning:**
- Always stay near line-of-sight breaks
- Pre-position for known mechanics
- Keep escape routes planned

</Aside>
