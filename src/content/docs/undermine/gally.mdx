# Chrome King Gallywix (Final Boss) - Heroic Raid Leader Guide

<CardGrid>
<Card title="Fight Overview" icon="information">
**Style:** 3-phase encounter  
**Duration:** 8-10 minutes  
**Positioning:** Melee range required  
**Bloodlust:** Phase 3 or intermission
</Card>

<Card title="Key Mechanics" icon="warning">
**Tank Swaps:** Every 8-9 stacks  
**Heroic Addition:** Dude Bombs  
**Critical:** Stay in melee range  
**Intermission:** Shield + Adds
</Card>
</CardGrid>

:::tip[Raid Leader Tip]
This fight heavily punishes ranged positioning - damage increases dramatically with distance from the boss. Keep everyone stacked in melee!
:::

## Phase 1 - Main Engagement (100-50%)

### <Icon name="approve-check" /> Trick Shots (Tank Mechanic)

<Steps>
1. **Stack Accumulation**: Tanks gain stacks every 4 seconds
2. **Monitor Stacks**: Watch for 8-9 stack threshold
3. **Execute Swap**: Taunt before reaching 10 stacks
4. **Position Away**: Face boss away from raid during swap
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="Normal Mode" icon="approve-check">
    - Stacks every 4 seconds
    - Swap at 8-9 stacks
    - Face away from raid
    - No persistent effects
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Heroic Mode" icon="warning">
    - <Badge text="NEW" variant="danger" /> **Persistent fire zones** left behind
    - Same swap timing (8-9 stacks)
    - Requires positioning coordination
    - Fire zones last entire fight
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

:::caution[Critical Warning]
**NEVER** let tanks reach 10 stacks! The cone damage at 10 stacks will likely kill multiple raid members.
:::

### <Icon name="rocket" /> Giga Coils

- **Trigger:** Boss reaches 100 energy
- **Effect:** Spawns electric coils around room
- **Damage:** Pulsing AoE from each coil
- **Duration:** Until destroyed
- **Priority:** <Badge text="HIGH" variant="tip" /> Ranged DPS focus

### <Icon name="external" /> Scatterblast Canisters

<CardGrid>
<CardGrid>
<Card title="Targeting" icon="magnifier">
**Targets:** 3-5 random players  
**Effect:** Large AoE explosion  
**Debuff:** Healing absorption shield
</Card>

<Card title="Heroic Changes" icon="warning">
<Badge text="HEROIC" variant="danger" /> **Larger explosion radius**  
Requires more spread distance  
Pre-position at max melee range
</Card>
</CardGrid>
</CardGrid>

### <Icon name="approve-check-circle" /> <Badge text="HEROIC ONLY" variant="danger" /> 1500-Pound Dude Bomb

<Aside type="danger" title="Raid Wipe Mechanic">
**Frequency:** Every 60 seconds  
**Mechanic:** Tank must interact to defuse  
**Timer:** 10 seconds to explosion  
**Failure:** Raid-wide lethal damage
</Aside>

<Steps>
1. **Bomb Spawns**: Look for visual indicator
2. **Off-Tank Response**: Immediately move to bomb location
3. **Interact**: Click/interact with bomb object
4. **Timing**: Must be completed within 10 seconds
</Steps>

## Intermission - Shield Phase (50%)

### Shield Mechanics Overview

<Card title="Shield Phase Details" icon="setting">
**HP:** 30% of boss health  
**Duration:** Until shield broken  
**Boss State:** Immune to damage  
**Add Spawns:** Continuous until phase ends
</Card>

### <Icon name="list-format" /> Add Priority (Kill Order)

<Steps>
1. **Darkfuse Technicians** - Crash mechanic near boss <Badge text="PRIORITY 1" variant="danger" />
2. **Overcharged Sentries** - 4-second beam casts <Badge text="INTERRUPT" variant="caution" />
3. **Wrenchmongers** - High melee damage <Badge text="TANK" variant="note" />
4. **Shield** - All remaining DPS when adds are clear
</Steps>

### Positioning Strategy

<FileTree>
- Boss Position (Center)
  - Crash Zone (Marked)
    - Safe Zone for Technicians
  - Raid Stack Point
    - Melee DPS
    - Ranged DPS
    - Healers
</FileTree>

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="Tanks" icon="approve-check">
    - Pick up Wrenchmongers immediately
    - Position near designated crash zone
    - Use personal cooldowns for add damage
    - Keep adds away from raid
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="DPS" icon="rocket">
    - Focus Technicians first (crash them safely)
    - Interrupt Sentries on cooldown
    - Switch to Wrenchmongers when available
    - Burn shield when adds are clear
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Healers" icon="heart">
    - Focus on tank healing during add phase
    - Dispel priority targets
    - Coordinate cooldowns for heavy damage
    - Prepare for Phase 3 transition
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Phase 3 - Execute Phase (50-0%)

:::tip[Phase 3 Strategy]
This is a burn phase! Ignore most mechanics and tunnel the boss. Save battle resurrections for this phase.
:::

### <Icon name="warning" /> Wholesale Slaughter

<Card title="Execution Mechanic" icon="warning">
**Type:** Raid-wide channel  
**Damage:** Increasing per tick  
**Duration:** Extends with each cast  
**Counter:** <Badge text="BURN BOSS" variant="tip" size="large" />
</Card>

## Role-Specific Strategies

<Tabs syncKey="role-guides">
  <TabItem label="Tank Strategy" icon="approve-check">
    ### Phase 1 & 3
    - **Positioning:** Always melee range - never back away
    - **Swaps:** Clean swaps at 8-9 Trick Shot stacks
    - **Bomb Duty:** Off-tank handles all Dude Bombs <Badge text="HEROIC" variant="danger" />
    - **Cooldowns:** Rotate defensives for each swap

    ### Intermission
    - **Add Control:** Pick up Wrenchmongers immediately
    - **Positioning:** Near designated crash zone for Technicians
    - **Survival:** Use personal cooldowns liberally
    - **Communication:** Call out add spawns
  </TabItem>
  
  <TabItem label="Healer Priorities" icon="heart">
    ### Healing Priority Order
    1. **Tank healing during Trick Shots**
    2. **Raid healing for Scatterblast explosions**
    3. **Absorb shield removal** (priority dispels)
    4. **Phase 3 Slaughter channels**

    ### Cooldown Usage
    - **Phase 1:** Conserve major cooldowns
    - **Intermission:** Use as needed for add damage
    - **Phase 3:** Rotate all major healing cooldowns
    
    :::tip[Healer Tip]
    Stack healing cooldowns during Wholesale Slaughter - the damage ramps up quickly!
    :::
  </TabItem>
  
  <TabItem label="DPS Responsibilities" icon="rocket">
    ### Target Priority by Phase

    **Phase 1:** 
    - Primary: Boss damage
    - Secondary: Giga Coils when they spawn
    - Positioning: Maximum melee range for Scatterblast

    **Intermission:**
    - <Badge text="1" variant="danger" /> Darkfuse Technicians
    - <Badge text="2" variant="caution" /> Overcharged Sentries (interrupt!)
    - <Badge text="3" variant="note" /> Wrenchmongers
    - <Badge text="4" variant="tip" /> Shield (when adds clear)

    **Phase 3:**
    - **TUNNEL BOSS** - ignore most other mechanics
    - Use all damage cooldowns
    - <Badge text="HEROIC" variant="danger" /> Help spot/call Dude Bombs

    :::caution[DPS Warning]
    In Phase 3, resist the urge to target adds - the boss must die quickly before Slaughter becomes unhealable.
    :::
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

<CardGrid>
<Card title="Positioning Errors" icon="warning">
<Badge text="FATAL" variant="danger" /> Tanking at range  
Massive damage increase with distance  
**Always stay in melee range**
</Card>

<Card title="Tank Swap Timing" icon="warning">
<Badge text="WIPE" variant="danger" /> Swapping at 10 stacks  
Cone damage hits entire raid  
**Swap at 8-9 stacks maximum**
</Card>

<Card title="Heroic Bomb Failure" icon="warning">
<Badge text="HEROIC" variant="danger" /> Missing Dude Bomb interaction  
10-second timer = raid wipe  
**Off-tank must be ready**
</Card>

<Card title="Add Priority Confusion" icon="warning">
Wrong kill order in intermission  
Technicians crash = raid damage  
**Follow priority: Tech > Sentry > Wrench**
</Card>
</CardGrid>

## Tips for Success

<Aside type="tip" title="Pro Tips">
- **Mark crash zone** for Technicians with world marker
- **Assign interrupt rotation** for Sentries (4-second cast)
- **Pre-position for Scatterblast** spreads at max melee
- **Use voice countdown** for tank swaps (3-2-1-TAUNT)
- **Save battle resurrections** for Phase 3 exclusively
</Aside>

### Recommended Addons

<Steps>
1. **WeakAuras** - Track Trick Shot stacks and Dude Bomb timers
2. **DBM/BigWigs** - Essential for ability timers and warnings  
3. **Details!** - Monitor positioning and damage taken
4. **Method Raid Tools** - Coordinate cooldowns and assignments
</Steps>
